# Marketing Launch Kit

Use this as the source-of-truth copy pack for launch week campaigns.

## Positioning

- Brand: NovaTech AI
- Category: AI-first automation consultancy with full product engineering delivery
- Core promise: remove manual operational bottlenecks fast, then scale with web, mobile, and SaaS execution
- Primary market: growth-stage teams and operationally heavy businesses in Ethiopia and regional markets

## Offer Ladder

- Entry offer: Workflow Audit
- Core offer: Custom agentic automation systems
- Expansion offer: Web, mobile, and SaaS product engineering
- Conversion assists: Instant Quote Wizard and Discovery Call booking

## ICP Segments

- Operations-heavy SMEs with repetitive handoffs, copy-paste workflows, and portal friction
- Service businesses needing faster lead response and proposal turnaround
- Teams with privacy requirements that need local or controlled AI deployment
- Businesses scaling digital products and internal systems in parallel

## Messaging Pillars

### 1) Throughput Over Busywork

- Headline angle: Stop manual work at the source
- Proof angle: show before/after cycle-time and processing-speed improvements

### 2) AI Plus Product Delivery

- Headline angle: AI automation first, product engineering in parallel
- Proof angle: web/mobile/SaaS track is not an afterthought

### 3) Privacy and Control

- Headline angle: private deployment options for sensitive workflows
- Proof angle: role-based access and reduced data exposure risk
